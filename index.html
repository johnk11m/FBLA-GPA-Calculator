Welcome to the GPA Calculator!

This tool allows you to calculate your unweighted and weighted GPAs.

  • 1. Choose whether you want to calculate your unweighted or weighted GPA.
  • 2. Click the "+" to add the courses/classes you are taking in a semester.
  • 3. Type your course name in the "Course Name" section, select the amount of credits the course has in the "Credits" section, and select the letter grade that you got in that course in the "Grade" section.
  • 4. After adding all your courses and its corresponding information, click "Calculate" to see your GPA.
  • 5. Use the "Reset" button to clear all entered data.
  • 6. Click "Grading" to look at the grading standard.
